ABOUT US

We are leading in the plants service fields.

We Indians have revered trees as gods throughout recorded history, and having trees in our homes has always been an essential aspect of our way of life. Giving trees as gifts has long been regarded as lucky by our ancestors. These behaviours diminished throughout time, though. By making numerous plants with both therapeutic and ornamental value accessible to people of all classes, we the HD Garden hope to revive this practise. HD Garden made sure to only utilise biodegradable products for the growth of the plants after learning about the effects of plastics.
